Markethunt API

Markethunt backend API documentation

Historical Marketplace Data

https://markethunt-api-dev.vsong.ca/items

Get information about every item on the marketplace and their latest price and volume.

Example

https://markethunt-api-dev.vsong.ca/items/{itemId}

Get information about an item and its marketplace history.

Marketplace Analytics

https://markethunt-api-dev.vsong.ca/analytics/total-volumes/{from}[/{to}]

Get total trade volume of all active items between two dates. The to date is optional and defaults to today.

Example

https://markethunt-api-dev.vsong.ca/analytics/movers/{from}[/{to}]

Get price movement information of all active items between two dates. The to date is optional and defaults to today.

Note: weekly volume is measured in the 7-day period preceding the to date and can be zero if the item has very low market activity.
